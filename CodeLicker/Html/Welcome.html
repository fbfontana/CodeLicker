Audit and fix with custom granularity

Renaming a variable is easy: right-click it and your IDE will apply that change to the whole solution. What if other variables need the same operation, e.g. to follow a custom naming convention? You don't want to click and rename 50 - 100 variables individually, right? Use CodeLicker and you won't have to; you will be able to pick if your action should be applied to a single file, its containing project or solution, the folder where you file resides... Of course you might want to get a report before opting for that change, so you can talk to other members of your team before acting. Many custom analysis can be performed by CodeLicker: do your methods violate your guidelines about length?
